REV-N-RIP HOME UX UPDATE

Changed:
- Added a sticky "I want to..." dropdown menu on the homepage.
- The dropdown immediately routes users to the action they want:
  Enter giveaway, find a card, make a private deal, sell/trade, shop, or account.
- Turned the top hero into a Giveaway First action block.
- Kept login-aware giveaway behavior:
  logged out = Log In / Create Free Account,
  logged in = Enter Giveaway,
  already entered = You're Entered.
- Applied to both Android app bundled site files and the website package.

The goal is fewer clicks and less navigation clutter.
