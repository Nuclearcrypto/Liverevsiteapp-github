Rev-N-Rip Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-12

This Privacy Policy explains how Rev-N-Rip collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Rev-N-Rip app, website, giveaway tools, marketplace pages, account features, Grail Radar, Garage Bay, and related services.

Information we collect

Depending on which features you use, we may collect:

  • Account information such as email address, username, login status, and profile details.
  • Giveaway information such as giveaway entries, entry timestamps, and prize eligibility status.
  • Shipping or mailing information you provide for giveaway fulfillment or account profile use.
  • Collection submission details, including descriptions of trading cards, sealed items, slabs, or collectibles you submit for buyout, consignment, trade, or review.
  • Deal-room, message, bounty, or marketplace activity you choose to submit inside the app or website.
  • Images or files you upload if image-upload features are enabled.
  • Basic device, browser, usage, and diagnostic information needed to operate and improve the app or site.

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Process giveaway entries and prevent duplicate entries for the same giveaway.
  • Contact giveaway winners and fulfill prizes.
  • Operate marketplace, bounty, sell/trade, message, and deal-room features.
  • Respond to collection submissions and customer inquiries.
  • Improve safety, reliability, fraud prevention, and user experience.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate Rev-N-Rip, such as hosting, database, authentication, email, analytics, fulfillment, or payment-related services. We may also share information when required by law, to protect users, or to prevent fraud or abuse.

Third-party services

Rev-N-Rip may use third-party services such as Supabase for authentication, database storage, and backend features. If marketplace purchases or external store links are used, checkout may occur through third-party platforms such as eBay, Shopify, or other payment/commerce services. Those services may have their own privacy policies and terms.

Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to operate the app, manage accounts, fulfill giveaways, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, maintain business records, and improve our services. Giveaway records may be retained to verify eligibility, winner selection, and prize fulfillment.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No online service can guarantee perfect security, so users should protect their login credentials and avoid submitting sensitive information that is not requested.

Your choices and deletion requests

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your Rev-N-Rip account information by contacting us at info.revnrip@gmail.com. We may need to retain certain records when required for legal, security, dispute, or legitimate business reasons.

Children

Rev-N-Rip is not intended for children under 13. Users should only use the app if they are old enough to create an account and participate in features under applicable law and platform rules.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
